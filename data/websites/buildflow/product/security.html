Security

Your ideas are safe with us.

Enterprise-grade security built in from day one — because founders share their most sensitive work with Buildflow.

🔒

End-to-end encryption

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256). Your ideas are never readable by our infrastructure staff.

🏆

SOC 2 Type II certified

Independently audited annually. Our controls for security, availability, and confidentiality meet the highest industry standards.

🇪🇺

GDPR compliant

Full GDPR compliance for EU users. Data residency options available. Request your data export or deletion at any time.

🤖

Never trained on your data

Your startup's data is never used to train AI models — ours or anyone else's. Period. This is a core commitment, not a setting.

🔐

SSO & MFA

SAML SSO, Google OAuth, and TOTP/hardware MFA supported. Enterprise SSO available on Scale plan.

📋

Audit logs

Every agent action, user access, and data change is logged. Scale plan includes full audit log export for compliance teams.

Responsible disclosure

We take security vulnerabilities seriously and appreciate the security community's efforts to help us keep Buildflow safe. If you discover a security issue, please email security@buildflow.app with a detailed description. We'll respond within 24 hours and keep you informed throughout the resolution process.

We do not pursue legal action against researchers who report vulnerabilities in good faith following this process.

Infrastructure

Buildflow runs on AWS (us-east-1, eu-west-1) with automatic failover. We maintain 99.9% uptime SLA on Pro and 99.95% on Scale. Infrastructure is isolated per customer on the Scale plan.
