How Buildflow works

Buildflow is organized around projects — each representing one startup or major initiative. Within each project, you have access to a set of specialized AI agents that share a unified context.

When you describe your startup (during onboarding or by editing your project settings), every agent uses that description as its foundation. The Product Agent knows your target customer. The Marketing Agent knows your positioning. The Sales Agent knows your pricing.

Project context

The project context is the brain of your Buildflow workspace. It includes:

  • Business description — what you build and for whom
  • ICP — your ideal customer profile
  • Competitive landscape — key competitors and differentiators
  • Tone & brand voice — how you communicate
  • Current stage & metrics — where you are and where you're going

You can update any part of the context at any time from your project settings. Agents immediately reflect the changes in their next outputs.

Agent runs

Each time you ask an agent to complete a task, that's one agent run. On the Starter plan, you have 3 runs per day. On Pro and Scale, runs are unlimited.

Agent outputs can be saved, edited, exported, or pushed directly to connected integrations like Notion or Linear.
