Buildflow is an AI-native operating system for founders. It gives you access to six specialized AI agents — for product, marketing, sales, finance, tech, and operations — that share a unified understanding of your startup. Instead of asking a generic AI assistant a question, you're working with agents that already know your market, customers, and goals.
General AI assistants are powerful but context-free — you have to re-explain your startup every time. Buildflow's agents maintain persistent project context, specialize by domain, coordinate with each other, and are trained on startup-specific patterns. They're purpose-built operating tools, not chat interfaces.
