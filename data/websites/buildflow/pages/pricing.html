Frequently asked questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes — all paid plans include a 30-day free trial. No credit card required to start. You're billed only when your trial ends and you choose to continue.

Can I change plans anytime?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade whenever you like. If you upgrade mid-cycle, you'll be billed the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the next billing date.

What counts as an "agent run"?

One agent run is a single task execution — generating a PRD, writing an email sequence, or running a market analysis. On Pro and Scale, runs are unlimited.

Are my ideas private?

Yes, completely. Your data is end-to-end encrypted and never shared or used to train AI models. We're SOC 2 Type II certified.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a full refund within 14 days of your first paid charge if you're not satisfied. No questions asked.

What integrations are included?

Pro includes Notion, Linear, and GitHub. Scale unlocks all 60+ integrations including Stripe, HubSpot, Salesforce, Jira, Figma, and more.
