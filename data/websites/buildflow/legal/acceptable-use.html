Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") describes what you may and may not do with the Buildflow platform. By using Buildflow, you agree to comply with this policy.

Permitted use

Buildflow is designed for founders and teams building legitimate businesses. You may use the platform to:

  • Build and manage your startup's strategy, product, and operations
  • Generate business content including PRDs, marketing copy, and outreach emails
  • Connect integrations with tools you are authorized to use
  • Collaborate with team members on shared projects

Prohibited use

You may not use Buildflow to:

  • Generate content that is illegal, defamatory, or designed to harm individuals or groups
  • Create spam, phishing content, or deceptive communications at scale
  • Impersonate other individuals, companies, or brands
  • Circumvent security controls or attempt unauthorized access to systems
  • Violate any applicable law or regulation
  • Generate content that infringes on third-party intellectual property
  • Use the platform to train or build competing AI products without explicit written permission
  • Resell, sublicense, or provide unauthorized access to the platform

AI-generated content

Buildflow's AI agents may sometimes produce inaccurate, outdated, or incomplete output. You are responsible for reviewing and verifying agent output before acting on it or sharing it externally. Do not rely on agent output for medical, legal, or financial decisions without independent professional review.

Enforcement

Violations of this AUP may result in suspension or permanent termination of your account without refund. We reserve the right to investigate suspected violations and cooperate with law enforcement as required.

Reporting violations

To report suspected AUP violations: trust@buildflow.app
