Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Buildflow, Inc. uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our platform. By using Buildflow, you consent to the use of cookies as described here.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help us recognize you, maintain your session, and improve the platform experience. We also use similar technologies like localStorage for session state.

Cookies we use

Essential cookies (always active)

These cookies are required for the platform to function. They cannot be disabled.

  • session_token — Maintains your authentication session. Expires when you sign out.
  • csrf_token — Protects against cross-site request forgery. Session-scoped.
  • workspace_state — Remembers your last active project and layout preferences.

Analytics cookies (optional)

Set only with your consent. Help us understand how founders use Buildflow so we can improve it.

  • _bf_analytics — Anonymized usage tracking. No personally identifiable information is collected. 12-month expiry.

Third-party cookies

When you connect integrations (e.g., Notion, Stripe), those services may set their own cookies governed by their respective privacy policies.

Managing cookies

You can manage cookie preferences from your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies will prevent you from signing in and using the platform. To withdraw consent for analytics cookies, visit Settings → Privacy in your workspace.
