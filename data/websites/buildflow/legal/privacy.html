Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Buildflow, Inc. ("Buildflow," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our platform.

Information we collect

Account information: When you sign up, we collect your name, email address, and password (hashed and salted — never stored in plain text).

Startup context: Information you provide about your startup — business description, ICP, competitive landscape, and goals — is stored to power your AI agents. This data is yours and can be deleted at any time.

Usage data: We collect anonymized data about feature usage, session length, and errors to improve the product. This data cannot identify you individually.

Payment information: Billing is handled by Stripe. We never store your full payment card details on our servers.

How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Buildflow platform
  • Personalize your AI agent outputs with your startup context
  • Send you product updates, security notices, and support responses
  • Comply with legal obligations

We never use your startup data to train AI models. This is a core commitment. Your ideas stay yours — always.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service providers who help operate our platform (AWS, Stripe, Resend) — bound by strict data processing agreements
  • Law enforcement or government bodies when legally required
  • Acquirers in the event of a merger or acquisition — you will be notified in advance

Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymized usage statistics may be retained indefinitely.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. GDPR users have additional rights including data portability and the right to object to processing. To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@buildflow.app.

Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and session management. Optional analytics cookies are only set with your consent. See our Cookie Policy for details.

Contact

For privacy questions: privacy@buildflow.app
