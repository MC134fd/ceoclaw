Accessibility Statement

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Buildflow, Inc. is committed to ensuring that our platform is accessible to people with disabilities. We believe every founder — regardless of ability — deserves access to the operating leverage Buildflow provides.

Our commitment

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. Our ongoing efforts include:

  • Keyboard navigability across all interactive elements
  • Sufficient color contrast ratios (minimum 4.5:1 for body text)
  • Meaningful alt text for all non-decorative images
  • ARIA labels and roles for complex UI components
  • Focus indicators visible on all interactive elements
  • Responsive design that works with browser zoom up to 200%
  • Support for screen readers (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver)

Known issues

We are actively working to address the following known accessibility gaps:

  • Some data visualization charts lack full screen reader descriptions — target: Q2 2026
  • Drag-and-drop interactions in the sprint planner do not yet have keyboard equivalents — target: Q3 2026

Feedback

If you encounter an accessibility barrier while using Buildflow, please contact us:

  • Email: accessibility@buildflow.app
  • We aim to respond within 2 business days

If you're not satisfied with our response, you may contact the relevant disability rights authority in your country for further assistance.

Testing

Buildflow's accessibility testing includes automated scanning (axe, Lighthouse), manual testing with VoiceOver and NVDA, and periodic audits with users who rely on assistive technology.
