Process

From chat to deployed UI in three steps

01

Describe your product

Tell the Studio what you're building — your audience, tone, and any specifics. Be as brief or detailed as you like. The AI asks clarifying questions if needed.

02

Review the live preview

A complete UI appears in seconds. Browse the live preview, inspect the code, and chat to iterate — change layout, swap fonts, adjust copy — until it's exactly right.

03

Export or deploy

Download clean files to continue in your own editor, or click Deploy to push to Vercel or Netlify with a custom domain in one step.
