Our story

Built for the founder
who does everything.

CEOClaw exists because the gap between a great idea and real revenue is mostly an execution problem — not a creativity problem. We're here to close that gap.

Mission

Give every founder the operational leverage of a full team.

Most great ideas don't fail because they're bad ideas. They fail because the founder runs out of bandwidth before they reach the market.

The solo founder is expected to do market research on Monday, build the product on Tuesday, write the website on Wednesday, send cold outreach on Thursday, and track the numbers on Friday. Without dropping quality in any of them.

The problem isn't that founders aren't capable. The problem is that this model is structurally broken. Execution bandwidth is finite. Cognitive switching is expensive. And the cost of doing any one of these things poorly compounds across all the others.

We built CEOClaw to give founders an execution layer that handles the structured, repeatable parts of building a company — so they can focus on the parts that only they can do. Taste. Judgment. Relationships. The creative leaps that no AI can replicate.

What we believe

  • 01 Execution bandwidth is the real bottleneck. The bottleneck for most founders isn't ideas, capital, or ambition — it's how much they can execute in parallel without losing coherence.
  • 02 Context is everything. Five isolated AI tools are worse than one system with shared memory. Coordination compounds value.
  • 03 Founders should stay in the high-leverage zone. AI handles the structured execution. Founders handle judgment, taste, and creative direction.
  • 04 Revenue is the only real signal. Everything in CEOClaw routes toward a measurable revenue goal — not vanity metrics.

How we build

Principles that shape every decision.

01

Coherence over features

We build fewer things that work together deeply, rather than more things that coexist loosely. Every operator must earn its place in the loop.

02

Measure what matters

MRR is the north star. Everything we build traces back to how it moves that number. Progress without revenue is not progress.

03

Respect the founder's time

Every interaction with CEOClaw should save time, not cost it. If a feature requires more cognitive load than it saves, we rethink it.

04

Transparent execution

Founders don't hand off to a black box. CEOClaw shows what it's doing, why, and what the output is — at every step of the loop.

05

Ship beats perfect

A working site, a sent sequence, a scored cycle — these beat a perfect plan sitting in a doc. CEOClaw is optimized for shipped output, not polished drafts.

06

Opinionated defaults

We make strong decisions on behalf of the founder — about structure, routing, format, and flow — so they don't have to configure their way to productivity.
