FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. The Starter plan is free forever — no credit card required. Upgrade only when you're ready to scale cycles and projects.

What counts as a "cycle"?

One cycle is a full pass through the CEOClaw loop: planner → router → operator execution → evaluator assessment. Starter plans include 8 cycles per month.

Can I switch plans at any time?

Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

What is the annual discount?

Choosing annual billing saves 20% compared to the monthly rate. The discount is applied upfront — you're charged the full annual amount once per year.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your project context, generated content, and operator outputs are scoped to your account. We don't use your data to train models.

What does "custom operator config" mean in Scale?

Scale users can set domain-specific prompting preferences, adjust operator routing weights, and configure the goal target per project — rather than using system defaults.
